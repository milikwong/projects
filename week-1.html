Weekly Make 01

With the time left that I had, I chose the topic of Health and Wellness because I have done work in the past that related to the topic. Also, so that I would have something to work towards for my first weekly make. Since there's many subcategories of Health and Wellness, I chose to do a healthy recipe card as a concept and formal approach, emphasizing simple and visually appealing presentation to experiment. My methodology approach was to not use traditional cooking-related shapes or symbols for the overall look.

I looked for different types of folds for inspiration and eventually mixed the idea of a flower bouquet and a recipe card together. I thought that if I were to give someone a healthy recipe card, what would be a nice way to present it? I have received many lovely flower bouquets in the past, it made me think that this was a fun way to gift someone.
